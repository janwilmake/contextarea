ContextArea Demo

ContextArea enhances your text editing experience by providing context for URLs, simplifying file sharing, and allowing easy paste & upload functionality.

Features:

  • Paste files/images to automatically upload them to pastebin
  • Drag & drop files to upload (hold Shift for regular paste)
  • Auto-detects URLs and shows context cards with previews
  • Click the upload button to select files
